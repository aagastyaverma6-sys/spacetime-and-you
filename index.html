Why is Space Getting Crowded?

Every rocket launch can place useful satellites into orbit, but it can also leave behind broken parts and old rocket stages.

Over time, these objects collect around Earth and become “space junk,” increasing the risk of collisions.

Types of Earth Orbits

LEO

Low Earth Orbit – used for imaging, ISS, and many internet satellites.

MEO

Medium Earth Orbit – mostly used for navigation systems like GPS.

GEO

Geostationary Orbit – used for TV, communication, and weather satellites.
