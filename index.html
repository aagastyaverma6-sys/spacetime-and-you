Why is Space Getting Crowded?

ORBITAL PROBLEM

Every rocket launch places useful objects into orbit, but old rocket stages, broken parts, and inactive satellites often remain in space.

RISK FACTOR

These objects become space debris and increase the chance of high-speed collisions that can damage satellites and future missions.

AI SOLUTION

AI can help identify dangerous orbital zones, predict collision risk, and suggest the best cleanup methods.

Types of Earth Orbits

LEO

Low Earth Orbit – used for the ISS, imaging satellites, and many internet satellites. It is the most crowded orbit.

MEO

Medium Earth Orbit – mostly used for navigation systems like GPS and global positioning satellites.

GEO

Geostationary Orbit – used for TV, communication, weather satellites, and long-duration observation.
